How It Works

1

Design

Use our Online Builder to explore customization options and select features that match your preferences. Create your unique instrument design with ease.

2

Order Submission

After all specifications are decided, save your Design and submit your order. Our team will carefully review your order to ensure everything is accurate. We'll contact you promptly if any clarifications are needed.

3

Construction

Once your order is confirmed, the construction of your instrument will begin. Throughout the build process and upon completion, we conduct thorough inspections to ensure your instrument meets our high standards.

4

Shipping

You'll receive an email notification once your instrument is ready to ship. If you paid a deposit, the remaining balance will need to be settled before we dispatch your order.

FAQ

How long does it take to build a custom guitar?

+

The build time for a custom guitar typically ranges from 3-6 months, depending on the complexity of the design and our current production schedule.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping costs and any applicable customs fees will be calculated at checkout.

Can I request custom inlays or artwork?

+

Absolutely! Custom inlays and artwork are available. Please contact us directly to discuss your specific requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For custom builds, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due before shipping.
